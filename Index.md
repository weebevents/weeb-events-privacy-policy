# Weeb Events — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 20, 2026

Weeb Events ("we," "our," or "the app") is a fandom event discovery app for the
Phoenix metro area (with Tucson included as a listing region). This policy
explains what information the app handles and how.

## 1. Overview

Weeb Events is built as a **guest-first app** — there is no sign-up, login, or
user account required to use it. Your saved events, alerts, follows, and
preferences are stored locally on your device and are not tied to any personal
identity we hold.

## 2. Information we collect

The app may collect limited, anonymous usage information — for example, which
screens are viewed or which general actions are taken (such as viewing an
event or tapping a ticket link) — to help us understand how the app is used
and to improve it. These do not include your identity or precise location. If
we adopt a third-party analytics provider, this policy will be updated to name
it.

## 3. Permissions the app requests

- **Location (approximate):** to measure how far events are from you for
  Fandom Radar. Optional — the app works without it.
- **Calendar:** only when you tap "Add to Calendar," to save that event to
  your device calendar. We never read your existing calendar entries.
- **Notifications:** to deliver Fandom Radar alerts. Optional.

## 4. Ticket links and third parties

Ticket and source links open the event organizer's or ticket seller's
website. Some ticket links may be affiliate links, meaning we may earn a
commission if you purchase — at no extra cost to you. Once you leave the app,
the ticket seller's own privacy policy applies. We do not see or handle your
payment information.

## 5. What we don't do

- We do not require or offer user accounts at this time. If an optional
  account or sync feature is added in the future, it will be opt-in and this
  policy will be updated before launch.
- We do not sell your personal information.
- We do not show third-party advertising.
- We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or precise
  location history.

## 6. Data retention and deletion

Because your settings live on your device, uninstalling the app removes them.
Push tokens and associated alert settings stored by our notification system
are removed when notifications are disabled or become inactive. To request
deletion of any data associated with your device, contact us at the email
below.

## 7. Children

Weeb Events is a general-audience event listing app and does not knowingly
collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change — for example, if we add optional
accounts, a new analytics provider, or new regions — we will update this
policy and revise the effective date above.
